# Natural Map Shape Pass V102

Goal: reduce hard grid/maze feeling without changing gameplay logic or solvability.

## Rules

- Do not change the maze graph in this pass.
- Do not block player corridors.
- Do not place decorative blockers in the spawn room.
- Do not overload every corner.
- Use sparse natural variation.
- Keep combat readability.
- Keep deterministic generation.

## Added visual language

- Rounded visual corner rocks.
- Soft edge boulders.
- Small ground patches.
- Mossy / earth / dry variants.

## Future final-map pass

This is not the final map. Future versions should replace grid-like generation with a designed level layout:
large rooms, natural turns, bigger combat arenas, biome zones, and final art assets.
