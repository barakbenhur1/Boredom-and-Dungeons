---
name: requirement-ledger
description: Preserve every user requirement through implementation and QA using immutable REQ ids; use for multi-part or regression-sensitive requests.
---

Extract atomic requirements as REQ-001, REQ-002, etc.
Keep the user's semantics; do not soften or expand them.
Map each requirement to owner, files, implementation evidence, and validation evidence.
Before completion, mark each as satisfied, blocked, or out of scope with a reason.
No requirement may disappear between planning and final report.
